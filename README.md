# VeggiEnoseData
VeggiEnose data

On 28/08/2025, measurements were carried out using an electronic nose in the quality laboratory of the Tomates del Guadiana factory with samples provided by the company. The different samples measured were:

Bag of tomato concentrate Brix 36–38 (control).

Tomato concentrate Brix 36–38 in which air had been introduced into the bag, suspected fermentation.

Expired tomato concentrate.

Fermented pulp.

For the measurements, 20 g of each sample were weighed. Each sample measurement consisted of an initial 2-minute cycle in the sample to achieve equilibrium with the volatile compounds, followed by a temperature sweep of 4 consecutive cycles from 400 °C to 200 °C following a triangular pattern. The total duration of these cycles was 8 minutes. Finally, a 14-minute desorption period was allowed so that the sensors could recover the baseline.

On the other hand, the company provided several samples to be analyzed in the sensor laboratory of the University of Extremadura. These were divided according to the following designation:

CB 36/38 (19:53) – Maillard reaction.

CB 36/38 (20:11) – Decreasing Maillard reaction.

CB 36/38 (20:14) – Further decreasing Maillard reaction.

CB 36/38 (20:22) – Even further decreasing Maillard reaction.

Measurements were performed with 2 minutes of adsorption and 30 minutes of desorption, carrying out 4 consecutive temperature sweeps from 400 °C to 200 °C. In addition, 15 g of each measured jar were weighed.
